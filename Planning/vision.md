# Vision

## Mission

We help growing businesses build reliable analytics systems that eliminate manual reporting, uncover operational insights, and give leadership the confidence to make data-driven decisions.

## Long-Term Vision

Build an analytics consultancy that partners with businesses to design, implement, and continuously improve their analytics capabilities.

## Guiding Principles

1. Business problems come before technology.
2. Build systems, not reports.
3. Automation is a multiplier.
4. Every insight should lead to action.
5. Simplicity scales.

## Portfolio Goal

This repository documents the design and development of a production-style analytics platform through realistic business case studies.
