# Enterprise KPI Framework

## Purpose

The Enterprise KPI Framework establishes the standardized business metrics used across Vespera Lifestyle Group. It defines a common language for measuring business performance, ensuring that executives, operational teams, and analysts evaluate the organization using consistent business definitions.

As Vespera Lifestyle Group expanded across multiple countries and sales channels, different departments began reporting conflicting revenue, inventory, and profitability figures due to inconsistent metric definitions. This framework eliminates those inconsistencies by defining enterprise-wide KPIs, assigning business ownership, documenting official calculation logic, and identifying the operational systems that supply each metric.

The Enterprise KPI Framework serves as the business contract between operational systems, the Enterprise Data Warehouse, and executive dashboards.

---

# KPI Governance Principles

Every KPI within the analytics platform follows five governance principles:

### 1. Single Business Definition
Every KPI has one official enterprise definition that is used consistently across all reports and dashboards.

### 2. Assigned Business Owner
Each KPI has a clearly identified business owner responsible for interpreting results and driving business decisions.

### 3. Standardized Calculation Logic
Metric calculations are implemented once within the transformation layer and reused throughout the analytics platform.

### 4. Traceable Data Lineage
Every KPI can be traced back to its originating operational systems and underlying business events.

### 5. Enterprise Reusability
Dashboards consume governed enterprise metrics rather than implementing their own independent calculations.

# Detailed KPI Specifications

## Net Revenue

**Business Purpose**

Represents the organization's official revenue metric used by executive leadership for financial reporting and strategic planning.

**Reporting Grain**

- Date
- Sales Channel
- Store
- Product
- Customer

**Enterprise Data Model Impact**

Requires detailed sales transactions, discounts, taxes, commissions, and return transactions at line-item level within the Sales Fact Table.

---

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Business Purpose**

Measures the efficiency of customer acquisition across paid marketing channels.

**Reporting Grain**

- Date
- Campaign
- Marketing Platform
- Acquisition Channel

**Enterprise Data Model Impact**

Requires integration of marketing spend with first-purchase customer events, combining Marketing and Sales subject areas.

---

## Inventory Turnover

**Business Purpose**

Measures how efficiently inventory investment is converted into revenue.

**Reporting Grain**

- Month
- Warehouse
- Product Category
- Brand

**Enterprise Data Model Impact**

Requires historical inventory snapshots together with Cost of Goods Sold to accurately calculate average inventory values.

# Relationship to the Enterprise Data Model

The Enterprise KPI Framework directly drives the design of the Enterprise Data Warehouse.

Each KPI determines:

- Required fact tables
- Required dimension tables
- Table grain
- Historical tracking requirements
- Business transformation logic
- Data quality validation rules

Rather than creating database tables first and defining metrics afterward, the Vespera Lifestyle Group analytics platform follows a business-first architecture in which enterprise KPIs determine the analytical data model.

---

# Key Takeaways

The Enterprise KPI Framework establishes a governed enterprise-wide measurement system for Vespera Lifestyle Group. By standardizing business definitions, assigning executive ownership, documenting official calculation logic, and connecting every KPI to its underlying operational systems, the organization creates a trusted foundation for enterprise reporting.

This framework completes the business architecture phase of the project and serves as the bridge between business requirements and the technical design of the Enterprise Data Warehouse.
