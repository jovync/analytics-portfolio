# Project Overview

## Executive Summary

Vespera Lifestyle Group is a fictional omnichannel lifestyle retailer operating across Southeast Asia. The company has experienced rapid growth over the past four years, expanding into multiple countries, sales channels, and operational systems. While the business has successfully increased revenue and market presence, its data infrastructure has not kept pace with its operational growth.

Today, business data is distributed across ERP, warehouse management, point-of-sale, e-commerce, marketplace, customer support, and digital marketing platforms. Each department maintains its own reporting processes, resulting in inconsistent business metrics, manual reconciliation, and delayed decision-making.

This project simulates a real enterprise analytics consulting engagement where a modern analytics platform is designed to unify operational data into a governed enterprise data warehouse. The platform provides executives with a single source of truth for reporting, operational monitoring, and strategic decision-making.

The objective is to demonstrate how enterprise analytics engineering, dimensional modeling, SQL development, and business intelligence can transform fragmented operational systems into a scalable, analytics-driven organization.

# Consulting Engagement

Vespera Lifestyle Group engaged our analytics consulting team because business growth had outpaced its data infrastructure.

Although every department maintained operational reports, leadership lacked a consistent view of business performance. Finance, Sales, Marketing, and Operations frequently reported conflicting numbers due to inconsistent business definitions, disconnected systems, and manual spreadsheet-based reporting.

As a result, executives spent more time validating reports than making strategic business decisions.

The goal of this engagement is to design an enterprise analytics platform that consolidates operational data, standardizes business metrics, and delivers trusted executive reporting.

---

# Business Challenges

The engagement focuses on solving several enterprise-wide business challenges.

### 1. Inconsistent Business Metrics

Different departments calculate revenue, profit, inventory, and customer metrics using different business rules. As a result, executive meetings often begin by debating which report is correct instead of discussing business strategy.

---

### 2. Fragmented Operational Systems

Critical business data is distributed across multiple operational platforms including ERP, POS, Warehouse Management, E-commerce, Marketplaces, Customer Support, and Marketing systems.

Because these systems operate independently, cross-functional reporting requires extensive manual reconciliation.

---

### 3. Inventory Visibility

Warehouse inventory synchronizes with online sales channels every four hours, creating temporary inventory discrepancies that lead to overselling, stockouts, and poor customer experience during peak sales periods.

---

### 4. Slow Executive Reporting

Monthly executive reporting requires combining exports from multiple systems using spreadsheets.

Preparing executive reports takes several days, preventing leadership from responding quickly to changing business conditions.

---

### 5. Limited Profitability Visibility

Marketing measures campaign success using advertising platform metrics such as ROAS, while Finance measures profitability using ERP data.

Without integrating operational and financial information, leadership cannot accurately determine which customers, products, and marketing campaigns generate long-term profit.

---

# Project Objectives

The Enterprise Analytics Platform has five primary objectives.

- Establish a single enterprise source of truth for business reporting.
- Standardize business definitions and enterprise KPIs across departments.
- Design a scalable dimensional data warehouse using Kimball methodology.
- Automate enterprise reporting through modern analytics engineering practices.
- Deliver executive dashboards that support strategic and operational decision-making.

---

# Expected Business Outcomes

Upon completion, the analytics platform will enable Vespera Lifestyle Group to:

- Reduce manual reporting effort through automated data pipelines.
- Improve confidence in executive reporting with standardized business definitions.
- Increase inventory visibility across retail and digital channels.
- Measure profitability consistently across products, customers, and marketing channels.
- Support faster operational and strategic decision-making using trusted enterprise data.
