# Enterprise Business Capability Model

## Purpose

The Enterprise Business Capability Model defines the core business capabilities required for Vespera Lifestyle Group to operate as a modern omnichannel lifestyle retailer. Rather than describing organizational departments or operational workflows, this model identifies the major business domains that create, manage, and consume enterprise data.

These capabilities establish the business foundation for the enterprise analytics platform and serve as the primary domains for data modeling, governance, KPI development, and executive reporting.

---

## Enterprise Business Capability Map

### 1. Master Data & Governance

Provides the enterprise-wide foundation for consistent and reliable reporting by maintaining standardized master data across products, customers, suppliers, stores, and organizational hierarchies. This capability resolves common data quality challenges such as duplicate supplier records, inconsistent SKU naming across channels, and conflicting business definitions, ensuring every department operates from a single source of truth.

**Business Value**

- Standardizes enterprise master data
- Improves data quality and governance
- Enables consistent reporting across all business functions

---

### 2. Omnichannel Sales & Commerce

Consolidates customer transactions across physical retail stores, e-commerce, regional marketplaces, and wholesale partners into a unified commercial domain. This capability standardizes revenue reporting across all sales channels while supporting customer purchasing analysis, pricing, promotions, commissions, taxes, and net revenue calculations.

**Business Value**

- Provides a unified view of enterprise sales
- Enables consistent commercial reporting across channels
- Supports customer and revenue analytics

---

### 3. Supply Chain, Inventory & Logistics

Provides end-to-end visibility across suppliers, warehouses, distribution centers, and retail locations. This capability monitors inventory availability, stock movements, fulfillment performance, and logistics operations while supporting replenishment planning, inventory allocation, and operational efficiency.

**Business Value**

- Improves inventory visibility
- Reduces stockouts and overstock situations
- Optimizes warehouse and fulfillment performance

---

### 4. Sourcing & Manufacturing Operations

Manages the complete product production lifecycle—from raw material procurement through manufacturing, quality assurance, and finished goods receiving. By integrating production, supplier, and cost information, this capability enables operational leaders to evaluate manufacturing efficiency, supplier performance, production costs, and product quality.

**Business Value**

- Measures production efficiency
- Improves supplier performance management
- Supports manufacturing cost optimization and quality control

---

### 5. Marketing & Customer Growth

Integrates marketing performance with customer purchasing behavior to provide a complete view of customer acquisition, engagement, and retention. By connecting campaign investment with downstream sales, profitability, and repeat purchasing, this capability enables accurate measurement of Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS), and Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).

**Business Value**

- Measures marketing effectiveness beyond advertising platforms
- Connects marketing investment to business profitability
- Supports customer acquisition and retention strategies

---

### 6. Customer Experience & Reverse Logistics

Captures customer interactions throughout the post-purchase journey, including customer support, product returns, exchanges, and reverse logistics. This capability measures customer satisfaction, identifies product quality issues, and quantifies the operational and financial impact of returns while feeding insights back into merchandising, manufacturing, and product development.

**Business Value**

- Improves customer satisfaction
- Reduces return-related costs
- Provides actionable feedback for product and operational improvements

---

## Relationship to the Enterprise Analytics Platform

Each business capability generates operational data that is integrated into the Enterprise Analytics Platform through governed data pipelines and standardized business rules.

Together, these six capabilities define the primary business domains that drive the enterprise data model, dimensional warehouse design, KPI framework, and executive dashboards developed throughout this project.
