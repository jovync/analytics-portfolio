# Enterprise Analytics Portfolio

A collection of end-to-end analytics engineering, business intelligence, and data consulting projects that simulate real-world enterprise engagements.

This portfolio demonstrates my approach to solving business problems through data—starting from stakeholder discovery and business process analysis, through enterprise data modeling, data engineering, and finally executive dashboards and decision support.

Rather than showcasing isolated SQL queries or dashboards, each project is designed as a complete analytics consulting engagement that reflects how modern organizations build scalable data platforms.

This portfolio demonstrates experience across the complete analytics lifecycle:

- Business Discovery & Requirements Gathering

# About This Portfolio

The scenarios presented in this repository are fictional but intentionally modeled after real enterprise environments.

Business processes, organizational structures, operational systems, and analytics challenges are designed to reflect the complexity commonly encountered in retail, e-commerce, supply chain, finance, and marketing organizations.

The objective is to demonstrate not only technical implementation, but also structured analytical thinking, stakeholder alignment, and enterprise-scale solution design.
